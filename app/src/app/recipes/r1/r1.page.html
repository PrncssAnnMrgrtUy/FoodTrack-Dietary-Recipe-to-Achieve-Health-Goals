Avocado-Egg Toast Try it once and we think you'll agree: Topping avocado toast with an egg is a near-perfect breakfast. Nutrition: per serving kcal: 271 fat: 18g saturates: 4g carbs: 18g sugars: 2g fiber: 5g protein: 12g salt: 0.2g Ingredients:
  • ¼ avocado
  • ¼ teaspoon ground pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 slice whole-wheat bread, toasted
  • 1 large egg, fried
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon scallion, sliced (Optional)
    •  Directions Step 1 Combine avocado, pepper and garlic powder in a small bowl and gently mash. Step 2 Top toast with the avocado mixture and fried egg. Garnish with Sriracha and scallion, if desired.
