Pork Steak Filipino beef steak, more fondly known as Bistek, utilizes this beautiful combination. But today, we’ll be exploring Pork Steak, which is a twist on that original recipe in using succulent pork chops as its meat.   • ½ cup liver spread

  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
